Childhood Obesity in Scotland

2015 Primary 1 Children by Weight Catagory

The UK is the most overweight country in all of Europe with 63% of adults being overweight or worse. With recent research showing that once you gain weight it is hard to lose it as your body resets its natural target weight.

So it is important for long term health that we address childhood obesity to catch these problem early. In the UK 1 in 10 children aged 5 to 19 years old are obese. This is three time the level found in other European country's such as Norway.

So How dose Scotland compare? Let's look at the data gathered over the last decade for Scottish Primary 1 children (typically 5 years old) at the start of there first school year. The good news is that in contrast to the 63% of adults that are overweight in 2015 this is only 15% for Primary 1 children. This is split into 9% overweight and 6% obese or worse.

The level of Primary 1 children being obese or severally has flat lined over the last 10 years ranging between 5% and 6% even though the number of Primary 1 children has doubled over that time.
